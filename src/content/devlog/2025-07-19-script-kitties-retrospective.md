What do people who have nothing to say write about? Lorem ipsum springs to mind, but
that's hardly worth your time to read let alone my time to copy-paste. Let's start with
introductions and see where that gets us.

Among other things, I'm the sole developer of **Script Kitties**, the action strategy
creature collector with deep turn-based combat. Script Kitties started in March 2024, when
I decided to kick off a 2-day game jam with everyone I knew. Only one person showed up — but
other people might have if I'd told them about it.

This is how it turned out:

![The result of the first game jam](/devlog/script-kitties-retrospective/gamejam.png)

Completing what I set out to do initially, I decided to turn it into something actually worth playing. Mostly, I was just enjoying programming again but after a few months, I started seeing a real game. I expanded existing systems, like rendering sprites flush with color, and added party-based combat to make things a bit more interesting.

![Party-based combat](/devlog/script-kitties-retrospective/combat.png)

The world was originally randomly generated across seven biomes.

![Randomly generated world](/devlog/script-kitties-retrospective/rng-world.png)

It turned out okay, and it was a lot of fun to implement, but I knew it would be difficult to
get right and chose to pivot to a hand-crafted world that felt more connected.

![Dialog and a more connected world](/devlog/script-kitties-retrospective/dialog.png)

More to come.
